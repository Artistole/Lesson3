My first blog

As I frantically hurry and try my best to finish the mark15 checklist, I am left with 2 things to do, the 1st one to finish styling mark14 and second one to write 2 blogs. I prioritized to write 2 blogs first, I look through Tanay's tweets and his own blog searching for topics and although Tanay did suggest writing a blog about tech and to document my journey, I chose to start writing by documenting my journey for the first blog.

I am studying in Bapatla Engineering college, although I have just started my 3rd year of IT, truth to be told I don't really have a lot of knowledge in terms of coding, I do have enough knowledge, but it's no ever close to some of the people i have seen in the neog.camp i wasnt baffled by this, obviously they are going to be more knowledgeable because I did not work hard enough, I did not even work to begin with. I wanted to change this, i wanted to be more knowledgeable, know more stuff about coding, now i had to choose either between front end or back end.

My math's is good but not good enough for advanced DSA and honestly front end was more interesting, so I wanted to try neog.camp levelZero and i started it amidst my 2nd year-end Sem exams so that I can give it quickly and I have never ever worked this hard or long to finish something, i have never put in consistent effort to work on something and never have I ever said no these many times to distractions. Whether it is because the course is taught by a really interesting and funny Tanay or because I have finally made up my mind to stop being such a dunce and do something productive, but I think it is both and either way i am here after 3 weeks with levelZero almost completely done, yes 3 weeks is a lot but i pat myself for this achievement because I have never put effort for this long into anything.

More blogs to come and from here on they will be tech related, mostly.
